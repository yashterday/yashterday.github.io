About me

Strategist | Analyst | Focused | Learner

ENTJ-A: Commander

Hi I'm Yash Tanna!

I'm currently an Information Systems & Economics Major at Santa Clara University. I plan on using the combination of my business skills and technical background to create solutions that will have a lasting positive impact on my community. I aspire to be a Business/Data Analyst or Product Manager, utilizing my experiences to provide the best possible solutions for the future.

In high school, I had the opportunity to lead my own small business, Insane Ink. I had a fantastic time during my two years with the company but as I graduated, there were two main take-aways from the experience. First, I enjoyed creating and developing products and getting them to the customers. There was no better satisfaction for me than to see a successful job with satisfied customers continuously coming back for more orders. Second, I love working in cross-functional teams. Being able to work with people from a variety of backgrounds has always interested me. Whether it was working with the sales people, organizing financials for our products, or just handling new marketing programs for the products, it was great to be invested in the process across all the steps.
